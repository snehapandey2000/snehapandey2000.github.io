Today's demanding technological environment, requires the switchboard manufacturer to supply design expertise and engineering that meets the customer's application and provide a competitive solution within the expertise budget and time frame.

Meeting these requirements demand assured and innovative approaches which Controls & Contracts has maintained throughout a history of highly successful projects.

  • With highly skilled, qualified, experienced team, proven procedures, modern equipment and the selection of reliable, high quality products, we are preferred to a large number of customers.
  • A superior computing system enables prompt, accurate quotations, AUTOCAD drawings, test records, job costing and financial performance.
  • Repetitive orders from our customers are indicative of the faith reposed by them inour expertise.

Electric Control Systems Specializes in design manufacturing and testing of commercial & Industrial switchboard & Plant automation systems which are customized to suit the specific requirements of the customer and installation. UPS range of switchboard are manufactured, assembled and tested by qualified trade personnel in accordance with BIS IS 8626/ IEC 60439.

The Company has the frame work for which switchboard are completely realized from the early stage of empty enclosure up to the end product, ending up with inspection and testing. The complete manufacturing process is carried out in accordance with ISO 9001:2008 quality assured manual.

We have a wide clientele including prestigious clients from various industries-IT, Mousing, Infrastructure Development, Power projects, OEM etc.
